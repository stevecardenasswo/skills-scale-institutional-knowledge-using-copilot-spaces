## Best Practices

### Communication Principles
- **Clarity**: Use clear, jargon-free language appropriate for audience
- **Consistency**: Maintain regular cadence and format
- **Timeliness**: Deliver communications as scheduled; escalate urgent matters immediately
- **Transparency**: Share both good news and challenges openly
- **Two-way**: Always provide opportunity for feedback and questions
- **Actionable**: Include clear next steps and owners

### Log Management Tips
- Update communication log within 24 hours of each interaction
- Review log weekly to ensure no follow-ups are missed
- Use communication IDs for easy reference
- Link related communications (e.g., follow-up to previous discussion)
- Archive completed communications quarterly
- Share relevant entries with Project Manager regularly

### Stakeholder Engagement Tips
- Tailor communication style to stakeholder preferences
- Prepare materials in advance of scheduled communications
- Follow up on commitments made to stakeholders
- Proactively communicate changes that affect stakeholders
- Build relationships beyond formal project communications
- Acknowledge and appreciate stakeholder contributions
